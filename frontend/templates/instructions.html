Design

InDeaTe is a Design Tool that enables the stakeholder in today’s effervescent innovation ecosystem to face these challenges, by empowering ideation through a methodical process of design.

Stages of Design

A ‘design’ evolves, from problem finding to solution seeking and finally to concept selection, along four stages - Task Clarification (TC), Conceptual Design (CD), Embodiment Design (ED) and Detail Design (DD)

GEMS methodology
Activities of Design

There are 4 activities that occur iteratively during each stage of design Generation of as many variants as possible of a solution Evaluation of the multiple solution variants generated, Modification of solution-variants with respect to Requirements, and Selection of the best solution among the variants.
