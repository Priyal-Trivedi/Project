Design Stage - Embodiment Design

This stage begins with the deliverables from Conceptual Design and ends with these deliverables:

  1. A more concrete list of requirements
  2. A list of possible solution feasible configurations that could be used to embody the solution principle
  3. An evaluation of these configurations for their suitability to satisfy the requirements
  4. The configuration selected as the most promising for further development.
