Design Stage - Embodiment Design

Activity - Evaluate, Select (ES/SS)


This stage begins with the deliverables from CD and ends with these deliverables: (1) A more concrete list of requirements; (2) A list of possible solution feasible configurations that could be used to embody the solution principle; (3) an evaluation of these configurations for their suitability to satisfy the requirements; (4) the configuration selected as the most promising for further development.
