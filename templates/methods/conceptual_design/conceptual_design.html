Design Stage - Conceptual Design


This stage begins with the deliverables from Task Clarification stage and ends with these deliverables:

  1. A more concrete list of requirements
  2. A list of possible solution principles that could be used to solve the problem (i.e. satisfy these requirements)
  3. An evaluation of these principles for their suitability to satisfy these requirements
  4. The principle selected as the most promising for further development.
